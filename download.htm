The University of Surrey is one of the UKâ€™s top professional, scientific and technological universities, with a world-class profile and a leading reputation in teaching and research. Most students at Surrey take up Professional Training Placements in their third year, working with partner organisations in the UK and overseas. Surrey has wide variety of departments across three faculties:

  • Arts and Social Sciences
  • Engineering and Physical Sciences
  • Health and Medical Sciences

The University of Surrey’s Widening Participation and Outreach department works to raise aspirations and attainment for students from a range of backgrounds, particularly those from under-represented groups. The Department also co-ordinates outreach for the Faculties and other areas of the University.
